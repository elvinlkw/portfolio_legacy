About Me


I graduated from the University of Waterloo in June 2018, with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering. As part of my coop undergrad program, I had the opportunity to work at different companies and develop skills such as computer programming, handling database systems, end-user support and hardware troubleshooting.

I am currently seeking a job where I would get to learn new technologies and grow as a Front-End Web Developer while contributing to the company's growth and meet the set deadlines.

I also like to work on personal projects whether hardware based or programming. In my spare time, you will probably find me hanging out with friends, playing soccer, looking for new music, or binge watching Netflix at home.
